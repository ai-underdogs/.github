## 🧠 Who We Are

Underdogs is an independent org founded on one simple belief: **the best time to build is now, and AI just made it even better.**

We're a scrappy crew shipping apps across every domain — productivity tools, games, creative utilities, wild experiments — using the latest AI tools to move fast and build things that are genuinely useful, fun, and worth your time.

We don't wait for permission. We don't have a corporate roadmap. We just build.

## ⚙️ Our Stack Philosophy

We don't have a single stack — we use whatever gets the job done. But a few things are constants:

- **AI-first development** — Cursor, Claude, Copilot, and whatever comes next
- **Speed over perfection** — ship early, iterate fast, listen to users
- **Small team, big output** — we keep teams lean and move accordingly
- **Open by default** — when we can open-source, we do
